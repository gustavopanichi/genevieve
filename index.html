Case Study

The Design Ecosystem

A 0→1 case study in design program leadership at Ring. Compounding, interconnected, strategic, portable.

01
UX Design Working Model
Foundation for team flow and clarity. A 0-to-1 operating framework including approach, philosophy, discipline flywheel, pod structure, and 12-phase lifecycle. 100% adoption across Ring, Blink, Key, and Sidewalk in 90 days.
02
Hybrid DPM Model
Scaled design leadership and alignment. Repositioned DPMs as strategic partners, not project trackers. 85%+ on-time delivery, 4.5/5 satisfaction, 90%+ sprint velocity.
04
Intake + Portfolio Intelligence
Customer-centered prioritization logic and real-time visibility. A unified portfolio model that made design work visible, strategic, and impossible to ignore. 40% faster design completion cycles.
06
XFN Cadence + Design Review
Team flow and consistent quality standards. Six rituals with one job each that replaced chaos with behavioral infrastructure. 100% cross-brand crit adoption, 35% fewer escalations.
07
Design Systems Integration
Consistent design quality and standards. Two design systems, two different problems, one philosophy. Adoption moved from 20% to 80%+ across 70,000+ designers at Meta.
09
XFN Engagement + Visibility
Clear ownership and observable impact. Co-creation as a philosophy about where ownership lives. 60% reduction in late CD engagement, 250+ hours saved quarterly.
